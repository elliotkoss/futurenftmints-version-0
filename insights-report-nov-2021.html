Nov 2021

The Future NFT Mints Insights Report is a daily analysis of one upcoming or currently minting NFT. These are published on Twitter and then added to this page, so if you want to always stay up-to-date on the latest Insights Report, follow @futurenftmints. DMs open.

BUILDERS: As soon as you have any details about your NFT mint, please use our NFT Submission Form to get on our radar. You can submit anytime you have new info, and we'll review it. Any submission with a public team will be automatically added to NFT Calendar (coming soon!), and you'll be given priority consideration for our daily Insights Report.
