NFT Trust Playbook

Created: Dec 21, 2021
Updated: Dec 21, 2021 - version 0.0.1

Future NFT Mints was created to provide in-depth analysis of upcoming NFT mints. During the 100+ hours of research plus 300+ NFT mints reviewed, some trends have emerged, and the biggest need that has consistently created challenges during the research process is establishing a baseline of trust signals that any NFT mint is able to execute quickly and easily.

These are the same 'trust signals' that users should use to evaluate whether an NFT mint should be trusted. Failure to identify who the team is should give you considerable pause before spending your money since best case you just lose your money but worse case your wallet gets drained by a bad actor. Trust is important to all of us.

The NFT Trust Playbook is mainly focused on trust () though there is one UX () tactic, and we've created a view-only Google Sheet with the checklist so that it's easy for any team to incorporate these ideas into their own NFT mint and product planning. Just copy it and use it.

This is the first iteration of the NFT Trust Playbook, and we fully expect it to evolve as we identify best practices. If something is missing, incorrect, or you just want to chat, please tweet @futurenftmints. DMs open.

Add tweet or URL proving @civickey
    AND/OR @proofofpizza verification (LINKS)
Have at least 1 community manager to answer questions and manage the community
Allow community managers to block users from specific channels
Allow community managers to block users from Discord server
Explain the qualifications for earning a spot on the Allow List
Require new users must read and accept rules for using Discord
Once rules are accepted, require new users must successfully do a Captcha
Do not require collab.land unless there is a specific NFT that a wallet must have to access something
Do not use any method of DMs for communicating with users
Explicitly tell users in every admin channel to NOT click any links that are not posted in #official-links
Do not allow any links to be posted in ANY channel except #shill
In rules, explain that all shilling (ie marketing other NFTs) is only allowed in the #shilling channel. One warning and then boot from channel.
In rules, explain that all sales (ie selling the NFT once it's been minted) is only allowed in the #sell-your-NFT channel. One warning and then boot from channel.
Do not allow any images to be posted in ANY channel except #shill and #show-your-NFT
Add Instagram to #official-links (if applicable)
Add Whitepaper to #official-links (if applicable)
Disable non-admin messages in INFORMATION channels
